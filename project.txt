Following the vessel along the route

Below are your best options, based on current (2026) market structure.

Provides real-time and historical AIS, very large ship database, and simple API.

B. Kpler AIS (MarineTraffic + FleetMon + Spire Maritime)

Currently the dominant end‑to‑end provider: terrestrial + satellite + 100+ nanosatellites after acquisitions.

C. ORBCOMM (independent satellite AIS provider)

Satellite + terrestrial AIS, still independent after market consolidation.
 [datapository.net]

D. NavAPI (low-cost AIS positions API)

For cost‑efficiency + quality: Datalastic or NavAPI.
For premium global coverage: Kpler AIS or ORBCOMM.

Dynamic camera angle
